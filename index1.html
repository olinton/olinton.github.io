Welcome to UW Actsci Club Website! UW Actuarial Science Club is an official student organization to serve UW actuarial science students by hosting variety of events such as resume & cover letter critique, mock interview, various tutorials, mentorship program, and social events. The club has a strong connection between students, faculty and companies. Every term we bring companies info-sessions to our members where our members will be able to talk and directly ask questions to employers. Everyone in UW is welcomed to join our club and share the resource. Networking is becoming more and more important and joining Actsci Club is one of the best ways to connect with exceptional students on campus.
